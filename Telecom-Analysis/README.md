he Telecom Analytics Dashboard is designed as a three-page analytical solution that provides comprehensive visibility into business performance, network quality, and customer-level data exploration, enabling both strategic and operational decision-making.

Page 1: Business Health & Customer Value
✅ Key KPIs
 Total Revenue
Total Customers
Active Customers
Outstanding Balance
Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)
Churn Customer Rate

Page 2: Network Quality & Operational Insights
To provide network and operations teams with deep insights into service reliability and infrastructure performance, with a focus on customers impacted by outages greater than 4 hours.
✅ Key KPIs
Total Outage Hours
Average Call Drop Rate
Total Tower Count
Customers Impacted (Outage > 4 Hours)

Page 3: Data Exploration
To enable users to self-serve telecom data and export information based on their specific business requirements.
